Transfer of ownership of an asset on AssetChain works in two steps:

  1. The AssetChain user wanting to receive the asset creates a Transfer request.
  2. The owner confirms the Transfer request.

To create a transfer request, you first need to know the Asset ID of the asset you want to gain ownership of. The Asset ID is shown on the details page of the asset.

Enter the Asset ID of the asset you want to receive. The Asset ID looks like this: a8499eb4a8499eb4a8499eb4a8499eb4.
